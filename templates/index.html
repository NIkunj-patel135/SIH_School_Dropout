Welcome to School Dropout Analysis Portal

School Dropout Analysis Portal intiative by Govt. of Gujarat.



The School Dropout Analysis Portal is an innovative and data-driven platform designed to address one of the most pressing challenges in education: student dropout rates. This digital tool serves as a comprehensive solution for educational institutions, policymakers, and researchers seeking to understand and mitigate the factors contributing to student disengagement and dropout.

Schemes for Students by Govt. of Gujarat

The Government of Gujarat has launched a number of schemes to benefit school students in the state. These schemes are designed to improve the quality of education, provide financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections, and support students in pursuing their academic and career goals.

Learn More

Vidhyalaxmi Bond Scheme

This scheme provides financial assistance to students from economically weaker sections to pursue higher education. The scheme offers loans at subsidized interest rates, and the government pays the interest on the loan while the student is studying.

Karmayogi Training Programme

This programme provides vocational training to students from Class 11 and 12 to help them develop skills that are in demand in the job market.

Chief Minister's Scholarship Scheme

This scheme provides scholarships to students from economically weaker sections who are pursuing higher education.

A Dropout Analysis Portal is a web-based platform that provides educators and researchers with tools to analyze dropout data. Dropout analysis is the process of examining data to identify patterns and trends in student dropout behavior. This information can be used to develop and implement interventions to reduce dropout rates and improve student outcomes.
